Cardio Zone

The cardio zone in the gym is dedicated to cardiovascular workouts, featuring equipment like treadmills, ellipticals, and bikes. It's ideal for improving endurance, burning calories, and boosting heart health.

Yoga Studio

A yoga studio is a tranquil space where individuals practice mindfulness, enhance flexibility, and build strength. Classes range from beginner to advanced, fostering community and promoting overall physical and mental well-being.

Weightlifting Area

The weightlifting area in a gym is equipped with barbells, dumbbells, squat racks, and benches, providing a space for strength training, building muscle, and improving overall physical fitness.
